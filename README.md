Global sales analysis of 1,328 orders across 46 countries  using Python, Pandas, and Seaborn to uncover profit trends,  shipping patterns, and seasonal insights.

# 📊 Sales Performance Analysis

## Overview
Analysis of 1,328 global sales orders from 2010 to 2017, 
covering 12 product categories across 46 countries in 
Europe and Asia. The goal is to uncover key business 
insights on sales performance, shipping efficiency, 
and seasonal trends.

## Tools & Libraries
- Python | Pandas | NumPy
- Matplotlib | Seaborn
- Google Colab

## Dataset
| File | Description |
|---|---|
| events.csv | 1,328 sales transaction records |
| products.csv | 12 product categories |
| countries.csv | Country and region mapping |

## Questions Explored
1. Which product category generates the highest profit?
2. Which country contributes the most revenue?
3. Which region has the strongest sales?
4. Is Online or Offline the dominant sales channel?
5. Which category takes the longest to ship?
6. Does shipping time affect profit?
7. What is the monthly sales trend over time?
8. Which day of the week has the highest sales?
9. Are there seasonal patterns in product sales?

## Conclusions & Recommendations
- Focus marketing on **Cosmetics, Office Supplies & Household** 
  — highest profit margin categories
- Strengthen presence in **Czech Republic** and 
  expand to neighboring Central European markets
- Invest in **online channel growth** to surpass offline sales
- Implement strategies to **break the sales plateau** 
  observed from 2010–2017
- Plan **Fruits inventory** around seasonal demand peaks
